About British Embassy in Dublin, Ireland

British Embassy in Dublin runs an inclusive range of consular services to local, British, and international citizens in Ireland.

The British Embassy in Dublin supports Irish citizens through its consular services, if they:

  • Need general or specific info regarding British economy, culture, sports, education and more
  • Need information regarding requirements and process of getting British Citizenship
  • Specific contacts and information in United Kingdom
  • Want to apply and obtain British Visa

Visa and Passport services

British passport and visa from are issued only to citizens whose main residence is in United Kingdom and Ireland.

The process of issuing British Visa and Passport of United Kingdom can take few weeks.

*Please contact with British Embassy in Dublin if you have any question as regards of visa and passport matters.

British representations in Ireland

British Embassy in Dublin is the only British diplomatic mission in Ireland.

    Irish representations in United Kingdom

    Country of Ireland has 3 other representations in United Kingdom:

    • Irish Consulate in Jersey
    • Irish Embassy in London
    • Irish Consulate in Edinburgh

    British representations worldwide

    British Embassy In Dublin represents one of 134 consular and diplomatic representations of United Kingdom all over the world.

    Foreign representations in Ireland

    British Embassy In Dublin represents one of 87 foreign consular and diplomatic representations from around the world in Ireland.
