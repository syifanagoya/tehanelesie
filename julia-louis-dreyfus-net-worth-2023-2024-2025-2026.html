Julia Louis-Dreyfus Biography

Julia Louis-Dreyfus was born in New York City on 11 January,1961, Her American born mother Judith, was a writer and special needs tutor, and her French-born father, Gérard Louis-Dreyfus, was in charge of the Louis Dreyfus Company. Her paternal grandfather, Pierre Louis-Dreyfus, was president of the Louis Dreyfus Group, he was a member of a Jewish family from Alsace and served as a cavalry officer and member of the French Resistance during World War II. She is also a great-great-granddaughter of Léopold Louis-Dreyfus, who in founded the Louis Dreyfus Group, a French commodities and shipping Commerce, which members of her family still control.
